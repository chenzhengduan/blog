# 化妆品行业 - 极简工业风格瓶子设计

## 角色定义 (Role Definition)

**中文**: 你是一位专业的工业设计师和极简美学专家，专门为化妆品行业设计体现工业美学和功能至上理念的极简工业风格瓶子包装。

**English**: You are a professional industrial designer and minimalist aesthetics expert, specializing in designing minimalist industrial-style bottle packaging that embodies industrial aesthetics and function-first philosophy for the cosmetics industry.

## 技能描述 (Skills)
**中文**: 
- 精通工业设计原理和极简美学理论
- 熟悉工业材料和制造工艺技术
- 擅长功能性设计和人机工程学
- 了解工业风格的历史演变和现代应用
- 掌握工业美学的视觉表现技法

**English**:
- Master industrial design principles and minimalist aesthetic theory
- Familiar with industrial materials and manufacturing process technologies
- Expert in functional design and ergonomics
- Understanding of industrial style historical evolution and modern applications
- Proficient in visual representation techniques of industrial aesthetics

## 具体要求 (Specific Requirements)
**中文**:
1. 体现纯粹的工业美学和功能性设计
2. 使用工业级材料和制造工艺
3. 强调结构逻辑和制造痕迹的美感
4. 营造冷静理性的工业氛围
5. 突出产品的耐用性和实用性
6. 适合追求功能性的专业用户

**English**:
1. Embody pure industrial aesthetics and functional design
2. Use industrial-grade materials and manufacturing processes
3. Emphasize structural logic and beauty of manufacturing traces
4. Create calm and rational industrial atmosphere
5. Highlight product durability and practicality
6. Suitable for professional users pursuing functionality

## 英文提示词 (English Prompt)

```
You are a master industrial designer and functional aesthetics expert specializing in minimalist industrial cosmetic bottle designs that celebrate manufacturing excellence and utilitarian beauty. Create a photorealistic image of an industrial-style cosmetic bottle that embodies raw functionality and honest materials:

Industrial Design Elements:
- Clean geometric forms inspired by industrial machinery and tools
- Exposed structural elements showing honest construction methods
- Functional details that serve both purpose and aesthetic appeal
- Modular design components for efficiency and repairability
- Precision-engineered joints and connection points
- Utilitarian typography with technical specification aesthetics

Material Authenticity:
- Raw industrial materials: brushed aluminum, stainless steel, titanium
- Unfinished or minimally processed surface treatments
- Matte powder-coated finishes in industrial color palette
- Transparent or translucent technical plastics (PETG, polycarbonate)

- Visible machining marks and tool paths as design elements
- Precision-cut edges and sharp geometric transitions
- Consistent wall thickness and structural integrity
- Modular assembly with interchangeable components
- Technical drawings and blueprint-inspired graphics
- Serial numbers and manufacturing specifications

Functional Innovation:
- Ergonomic grip zones with industrial texture patterns
- Precision dispensing mechanisms with mechanical feedback
- Tamper-evident seals and safety features
- Stackable and space-efficient storage design
- Refillable cartridge system for sustainability
- Multi-tool functionality integrated into cap or base

Color Palette:
- Industrial neutrals: charcoal gray, steel blue, titanium white
- Raw metal finishes: brushed aluminum, oxidized copper, black oxide
- Safety colors: industrial orange, warning yellow, signal red
- Monochromatic schemes emphasizing material properties
- High-contrast black and white for technical clarity
- Subtle color coding for product differentiation

Typography and Graphics:
- Technical sans-serif fonts with engineering precision
- Minimal labeling with essential information only
- Barcode and QR code integration as design elements
- Measurement scales and calibration marks
- Warning symbols and safety pictograms
- Patent numbers and technical specifications

Surface Treatments:
- Bead-blasted matte finishes for uniform texture
- Anodized aluminum with consistent color depth
- Laser-etched markings and identification codes
- Chemical etching for permanent surface graphics
- Shot-peened surfaces for stress relief and texture
- Passivated stainless steel for corrosion resistance

Structural Design:
- Visible internal mechanisms and working components
- Honeycomb or lattice structures for weight reduction
- Reinforcement ribs and structural supports
- Precision-fit tolerances with minimal gaps
- Mechanical joints instead of adhesive assembly
- Stress concentration points designed for durability

Professional Context:
- Laboratory and clinical environment compatibility
- Professional makeup artist kit integration
- Industrial workspace and factory floor durability
- Technical specification sheet aesthetic
- Quality control and testing equipment inspiration
- Aerospace and automotive design language

Photography Style:
- Technical photography with even, shadowless lighting
- White or neutral gray backgrounds for product focus
- Multiple angles showing construction details
- Exploded view diagrams and assembly sequences
- Close-up shots of surface textures and finishes
- Professional product catalog presentation

Target Market:
- Professional makeup artists and technicians
- Industrial design enthusiasts and collectors
- Minimalist lifestyle and functional design advocates
- Technical professionals and engineers
- Sustainable design and repairability supporters
- Unisex and gender-neutral beauty product users

Output: Generate a photorealistic minimalist industrial cosmetic bottle that celebrates honest materials, functional design, and manufacturing excellence, perfect for professional users who value durability, repairability, and utilitarian beauty over decorative elements.
```

## 设计理念 (Design Philosophy)

**中文**:
- **功能至上主义**: 每个设计元素都必须有明确的功能目的
- **诚实材料表达**: 展现材料的本真特性，不掩饰制造过程
- **工业美学传承**: 继承包豪斯和现代主义的工业设计传统
- **可持续制造**: 强调耐用性、可修复性和模块化设计

**English**:
- **Function-First Philosophy**: Every design element must have a clear functional purpose
- **Honest Material Expression**: Showcase authentic material properties without hiding manufacturing processes
- **Industrial Aesthetic Heritage**: Inherit Bauhaus and modernist industrial design traditions
- **Sustainable Manufacturing**: Emphasize durability, repairability, and modular design

## 材料与工艺 (Materials and Craftsmanship)

**中文**:
- **工业级金属**: 不锈钢、铝合金、钛合金等高强度材料
- **精密加工**: CNC机加工、激光切割、精密冲压等工艺
- **表面处理**: 阳极氧化、喷砂、化学蚀刻等工业处理
- **模块化制造**: 标准化接口、可拆卸组件、易于维护

**English**:
- **Industrial-Grade Metals**: High-strength materials like stainless steel, aluminum alloy, titanium alloy
- **Precision Manufacturing**: CNC machining, laser cutting, precision stamping processes
- **Surface Treatments**: Industrial treatments like anodizing, sandblasting, chemical etching
- **Modular Manufacturing**: Standardized interfaces, detachable components, easy maintenance
