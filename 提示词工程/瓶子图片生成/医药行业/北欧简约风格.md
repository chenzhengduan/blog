## 英文提示词

Create a Nordic minimalist pharmaceutical bottle design with the following specifications:

**Design Philosophy**: Scandinavian hygge meets medical functionality - warm minimalism with sustainable consciousness

**Shape & Structure**:
- Organic, flowing forms inspired by natural elements (pebbles, seeds, water drops)
- Soft, rounded edges with gentle curves for comfortable handling
- Human-scale proportions that feel natural in the hand
- Integrated functional elements that blend seamlessly with the form
- Stackable design with subtle interlocking features
- Ergonomic grip zones with natural finger placement

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Warm white (#F8F6F0, #FEFEFE) and soft gray (#E8E6E1)
- Natural: Light birch (#F5F1E8) and pale oak (#E6DDD4)
- Accent: Sage green (#9CAF88) and dusty blue (#A8B5C1)
- Earth tones: Sand (#D4C4A8) and stone gray (#B8AFA5)
- Fresh: Mint (#E8F4F0) and powder pink (#F2E8E5)

**Materials & Finishes**:
- Sustainable bio-based plastics or bamboo fiber composites
- Recycled glass with smooth, tactile finish
- Natural cork accents for caps or decorative elements
- Water-based, non-toxic surface treatments
- Soft-touch matte finishes that feel warm to the touch
- FSC-certified wood elements where applicable

**Nordic Elements**:
- Clean, uncluttered surfaces with generous negative space
- Subtle wood grain textures or natural fiber patterns
- Minimalist typography using Nordic-inspired fonts (Futura, Avenir)
- Small, thoughtful details that enhance functionality
- Integration of natural light and shadow play in the design
- Honest material expression without artificial embellishment

**Functional Features**:
- Intuitive opening mechanisms that require no explanation
- Child-safe designs that don't compromise adult usability
- Clear dosage indicators integrated into the design language
- Comfortable grip for users of all ages and abilities
- Tamper-evident features that maintain design integrity
- Easy-to-read labeling with high contrast but soft appearance

**Sustainability Features**:
- Fully recyclable or compostable materials
- Minimal packaging waste through efficient design
- Refillable design options where appropriate
- Local material sourcing indicators
- Carbon footprint reduction through lightweight construction
- End-of-life disposal instructions clearly marked

**Typography & Labeling**:
- Clean, humanist sans-serif fonts with excellent readability
- Generous letter spacing and line height for clarity
- Multilingual capability with Nordic language considerations
- Subtle hierarchy using size and weight rather than color
- Integration of universal symbols for international use
- Tactile elements for visually impaired users

**Cultural Integration**:
- Reflection of Nordic values: simplicity, functionality, sustainability
- Connection to nature through organic forms and natural materials
- Emphasis on well-being and holistic health approaches
- Democratic design principles - accessible to all users
- Seasonal adaptability reflecting Nordic climate awareness
- Community and family-oriented design considerations

**Environmental Context**:
- Natural lighting scenarios (Nordic daylight, candlelight)
- Home environment settings emphasizing comfort and warmth
- Healthcare settings that feel welcoming rather than clinical
- Seasonal contexts showing adaptability to Nordic lifestyle

Render in soft, natural lighting that emphasizes the tactile qualities and sustainable nature of the materials. Show the bottle in both clinical and home environments, highlighting its versatility and human-centered design approach.

## 中文翻译

创建一个北欧简约医药瓶子设计，具有以下规格：

**设计哲学**：斯堪的纳维亚hygge理念与医疗功能性的结合 - 温暖的极简主义与可持续意识

**造型结构**：
- 受自然元素启发的有机流动形态（鹅卵石、种子、水滴）
- 柔和圆润的边缘，带有温和曲线，便于舒适握持
- 手感自然的人体比例尺度
- 与形态无缝融合的集成功能元素
- 带有微妙互锁功能的可堆叠设计
- 符合自然手指放置的人体工程学握持区域

**色彩搭配**：
- 主色：温暖白色（#F8F6F0, #FEFEFE）和柔和灰色（#E8E6E1）
- 自然色：浅桦木色（#F5F1E8）和淡橡木色（#E6DDD4）
- 点缀色：鼠尾草绿（#9CAF88）和灰蓝色（#A8B5C1）
- 大地色调：沙色（#D4C4A8）和石灰色（#B8AFA5）
- 清新色：薄荷色（#E8F4F0）和粉色（#F2E8E5）

**材质工艺**：
- 可持续生物基塑料或竹纤维复合材料
- 回收玻璃，光滑触感饰面
- 天然软木装饰，用于瓶盖或装饰元素
- 水性、无毒表面处理
- 触感温暖的柔软哑光饰面

**北欧元素**：
- 清洁、整齐的表面，充足的负空间

- 使用北欧风格字体的极简排版（Futura、Avenir）
- 增强功能性的小而贴心的细节
- 设计中自然光影的整合
- 诚实的材料表达，无人工装饰

**功能特性**：
- 无需解释的直观开启机制
- 不影响成人使用性的儿童安全设计
- 融入设计语言的清晰剂量指示器
- 适合所有年龄和能力用户的舒适握持
- 保持设计完整性的防篡改功能
- 高对比度但外观柔和的易读标签

**可持续特性**：
- 完全可回收或可堆肥材料
- 通过高效设计最小化包装废料
- 适当时的可重复填充设计选项
- 本地材料采购指标
- 通过轻量化结构减少碳足迹
- 清晰标记的生命周期结束处置说明

**排版标识**：
- 清洁、人文主义无衬线字体，具有出色的可读性
- 充足的字母间距和行高，确保清晰度
- 考虑北欧语言的多语言能力
- 使用大小和重量而非颜色的微妙层次

**文化整合**：
- 反映北欧价值观：简洁、功能性、可持续性
- 通过有机形态和天然材料与自然连接
- 强调健康和整体健康方法
- 民主设计原则 - 所有用户都可使用
- 反映北欧气候意识的季节适应性
- 以社区和家庭为导向的设计考虑

**环境背景**：
- 自然照明场景（北欧日光、烛光）
- 强调舒适和温暖的家庭环境设置
- 感觉温馨而非临床的医疗环境
- 显示对北欧生活方式适应性的季节背景

以强调材料触觉品质和可持续性的柔和自然照明渲染。展示瓶子在临床和家庭环境中的应用，突出其多功能性和以人为中心的设计方法。
