## 医药包装标准 (Pharmaceutical Packaging Standards)

**中文**:
- **材料标准**: USP Class VI医用级塑料、药用玻璃、铝箔密封
- **安全标准**: 儿童防护包装、防篡改设计、毒物预防
- **标识要求**: 批号、有效期、条形码、警告标识
- **颜色编码**: 琥珀色（避光）、白色（一般）、蓝色（注射用）
- **法规遵循**: FDA、EMA、ICH、GMP标准

**English**:
- **Material Standards**: USP Class VI medical grade plastic, pharmaceutical glass, aluminum foil sealing
- **Safety Standards**: Child-resistant packaging, tamper-evident design, poison prevention
- **Labeling Requirements**: Batch numbers, expiration dates, barcodes, warning labels
- **Color Coding**: Amber (light protection), white (general), blue (injection)
- **Regulatory Compliance**: FDA, EMA, ICH, GMP standards

## 功能设计要素 (Functional Design Elements)

**中文**:
- **人体工程学**: 防滑握持、易开启设计、老年友好
- **剂量控制**: 精确分配、防过量设计、计量标记
- **存储优化**: 堆叠设计、空间效率、运输安全
- **环境适应**: 温湿度稳定、化学兼容、光照保护
- **使用便利**: 单手操作、视觉辅助、触觉反馈

**English**:
- **Ergonomics**: Non-slip grip, easy-open design, elderly-friendly
- **Dosage Control**: Precise dispensing, overdose prevention, measurement markings
- **Storage Optimization**: Stackable design, space efficiency, transport safety
- **Environmental Adaptation**: Temperature/humidity stability, chemical compatibility, light protection
- **Usage Convenience**: One-handed operation, visual aids, tactile feedback

## 适用产品类型 (Applicable Product Types)

**中文**:
- **处方药**: 片剂、胶囊、液体制剂
- **非处方药**: 维生素、止痛药、感冒药
- **生物制品**: 疫苗、血液制品、蛋白质药物
- **特殊制剂**: 儿童用药、老年用药、急救药品
- **医疗器械**: 试剂、诊断用品、医用耗材

**English**:
- **Prescription Drugs**: Tablets, capsules, liquid formulations
- **OTC Medications**: Vitamins, pain relievers, cold medicines
- **Biologics**: Vaccines, blood products, protein drugs
- **Special Formulations**: Pediatric drugs, geriatric medications, emergency medicines
- **Medical Devices**: Reagents, diagnostic supplies, medical consumables
