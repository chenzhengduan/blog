## 工业设计原则 (Industrial Design Principles)

**中文**:
- **形式追随功能**: 每个设计元素都有明确的功能目的
- **材料诚实**: 展现材料本身的特性和美感
- **制造逻辑**: 设计体现生产工艺和装配逻辑
- **系统思维**: 考虑产品在整个系统中的作用
- **用户中心**: 以用户需求和体验为设计核心
- **可持续性**: 考虑环境影响和生命周期

**English**:
- **Form Follows Function**: Every design element has a clear functional purpose
- **Material Honesty**: Showcase the inherent properties and beauty of materials
- **Manufacturing Logic**: Design reflects production processes and assembly logic
- **Systems Thinking**: Consider the product's role in the overall system
- **User-Centered**: Design core based on user needs and experience
- **Sustainability**: Consider environmental impact and lifecycle
